Notes

• One TradingView alert per strategy. The alert sends a single strategy_id; the middleware fans out to every enabled venue.
• Symbol is computed, not configured. Add new exchanges by extending EXCHANGE_QUOTE_SUFFIX in app/routing.py.
• Per-venue toggles persist instantly and reload the in-memory router.
• A strategy with all venues disabled is preserved in the YAML but no orders fire — useful for pausing without losing the row.
• Rotate the secret with flyctl secrets set WEBHOOK_SECRET=$(openssl rand -hex 32) then update TradingView's alert body to match.
