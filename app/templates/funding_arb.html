Net = funding − commission + Dir. net + Realized (total economic value). A closed leg books its realized directional P&L at the close mark into Realized, so Net keeps it — the live MTM (Dir. net) is suppressed once flat, never double-counted. Basis = the spread captured at entry (perp short avg − spot long avg, realizes as the basis converges) — shown for reference, NOT double-booked into Net. Funding accrues on the PERP leg only (spot shows 0). Spot/perp unrealized are cost-basis marks; Dir. net (their sum) sits ≈0 on a balanced pair — a large value (or the skew badge) flags broken neutrality.
